About Indranil Basu

Indranil Basu is a seasoned sports journalist specializing in cricket, renowned for his sharp cricketing intelligence and profound understanding of the game's intricate power structures. With over two decades of experience covering cricket and cricketers, he has built a reputation for going far beyond conventional match reports. His insightful work decodes critical elements such as selection strategies, leadership dynamics, dressing-room culture, and the administrative forces shaping Indian cricket.

Drawing on credible sources and delivering fearless, clear analysis, Indranil expertly tracks the intersection of on-field performance and off-field politics—whether it's a high-profile captaincy change, a generational shift in the team, or a pivotal strategic decision that alters a side's trajectory. His writing seamlessly blends exclusive access with in-depth analysis, empowering readers and fans to not only know **what** happened in Indian cricket but to truly understand **why** it happened.

Currently contributing to platforms like Sportskeeda (where he has broken major stories) and previously associated with The Times of India among others, Indranil continues to provide authoritative, behind-the-scenes perspectives on the sport's biggest developments.
